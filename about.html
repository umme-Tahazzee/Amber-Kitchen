Meet the Visionary

Chef Marco
Ferretti

Born in Florence, Italy, Marco Ferretti discovered his love for cooking in his grandmother's kitchen at the age of seven. After training under legendary chefs in Paris, Milan, and Tokyo, he brought his unique cross-cultural vision to New York City in 2010.

Today, Chef Marco holds three Michelin stars and has been named one of the world's 50 Best Chefs for five consecutive years. His philosophy is rooted in seasonal, locally-sourced ingredients transformed through classical European technique.

Our Journey

From Florence
to Fifth Avenue

Fifteen years of milestones, awards, and unforgettable evenings — a brief look at how we became what we are today.

2010

Savoria Opens Its Doors

Chef Marco opens a 40-seat restaurant on West 58th Street with a handwritten menu and a borrowed espresso machine.

2013

First Michelin Star

Three years in, the Michelin Guide awards Savoria its first star — the beginning of a remarkable recognition streak.

2015

Three Stars & Expansion

Savoria earns its third Michelin star and expands to a 120-seat dining room with a dedicated tasting counter.

2019

World's 50 Best Debut

Chef Marco enters the prestigious list at number 22, drawing guests from over 60 countries.

2025

Savoria Today

Now 15 years strong, redefining fine dining with a seasonal tasting menu, private dining room, and culinary masterclasses.
